Key Risks

Investment Thesis

GM Factory ZERO produces the Hummer EV, Chevrolet Silverado EV, GMC Sierra EV, and Cadillac Escalade IQ at its $2.2B Detroit-Hamtramck facility with 2,200+ employees. However, Hummer EV and Escalade IQ production was halted through end of 2025 due to slower-than-expected EV demand, affecting approximately 280 employees. A January-February 2025 pause for paint shop upgrades preceded the October production halt announcement. GM became the #2 U.S. EV seller in H2 2024 behind Tesla. The facility represents GM's flagship EV assembly plant. (Sources: GM Investor Relations; Detroit Free Press Oct 2025)
