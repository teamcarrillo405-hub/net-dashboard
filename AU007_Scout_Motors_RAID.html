Investment Thesis

Scout Motors represents a strong execution profile in the EV sector. Construction of the $2.3B Blythewood, South Carolina facility is proceeding on schedule for late 2027 production. As of September 2025, the 1.3-million-square-foot main assembly building is enclosed, with three supplier park structures under construction. Scout revealed the Traveler SUV and Terra Truck in October 2024 and has received 50,000+ reservations. A September 2025 announcement added a $300M supplier park. Target employment is 4,000+. (Sources: Scout Motors Blog May/Oct/Nov 2025)
