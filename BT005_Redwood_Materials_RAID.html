Investment Thesis

Redwood Materials operates the largest battery recycling facility in North America at its McCarran, Nevada campus with 500+ employees. Founded by former Tesla CTO JB Straubel, the company raised $1B in 2023 and secured $2B DOE loan commitment. Redwood processes 30+ GWh of batteries annually, recovering 95%+ of critical materials (lithium, nickel, cobalt, copper). The company supplies cathode active materials to Panasonic and battery manufacturers. A $3.5B South Carolina campus was announced in 2022. Redwood partners with Ford, Toyota, Volkswagen, and Volvo for end-of-life battery collection. (Sources: Redwood Materials; DOE Loan Programs Office)
