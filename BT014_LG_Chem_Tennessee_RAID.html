Investment Thesis

LG Chem Tennessee represents the largest cathode manufacturing facility in the United States and the single largest foreign direct investment in Tennessee history. The project benefits from strong strategic positioning in the heart of the U.S. EV supply chain with proximity to major automakers including Toyota, GM, and Ford. The $2.5 billion long-term supply agreement with Toyota provides revenue visibility while the 100% renewable energy operation aligns with automaker sustainability requirements. Phase 1 construction completion and equipment installation progressing on schedule positions the facility for 2026 production start. The 20-year PILOT agreement with Montgomery County provides favorable tax treatment while the IRA eligibility enhances customer economics. Rating: BUY. (Sources: LG Chem filings, Tennessee ECD, Clarksville permits)
