Investment Thesis

Amazon AWS committed to major expansion totaling over $108B: $20B Pennsylvania AI Innovation Campuses; $10B North Carolina Richmond County expansion; $15B Indiana (December 2025); $13B Mississippi; up to $50B U.S. Government AI infrastructure. The Talen Energy nuclear deal expanded from 960 MW to 1,920 MW in a 17-year, $18B PPA for the Susquehanna plant in Pennsylvania. FERC rejected the original behind-the-meter arrangement in November 2024, requiring restructuring. 2025 CapEx is $100-125B. (Sources: Data Center Frontier; Utility Dive; CNBC Nov 2025; POWER Magazine)
