Investment Thesis

CoreWeave operates GPU cloud infrastructure at its Denton, Texas data center through a $12B Microsoft Azure contract. The company raised $7.5B debt financing in 2024, the largest private credit deal for a tech company. CoreWeave filed for IPO in late 2024 with $2B revenue run rate. Core Scientific hosts CoreWeave at its Denton facility under a $6.6B 200+ MW hosting agreement. CoreWeave specializes in Nvidia GPU clusters for AI training and inference, serving OpenAI, Microsoft, and other AI companies. ERCOT grid constraints and Texas power availability remain key infrastructure challenges. (Sources: CoreWeave S-1 Filing; Core Scientific; Data Center Dynamics)
