Jobs creation, regional economic effects, and supply chain development across tracked projects

The Semiconductor Industry Association reports that since 2020, CHIPS Act and related investments have driven $630 billion across 140 projects in 28 states. Arizona leads with $97.5 billion in committed investment and 11,000+ projected jobs from semiconductor projects alone. However, project delays at Intel Ohio and others mean ~40,000 promised jobs remain unrealized.
