Water Resource Considerations
Semiconductor Fab Water Usage
Advanced chip fabs require ultra-pure water (UPW) for wafer cleaning. A single large fab can use 2-5 million gallons daily, comparable to a small city. TSMC Arizona has secured water rights and built advanced recycling systems.
