Administration Change Risk
The Trump administration is exploring converting CHIPS Act grants to equity stakes in recipient companies including TSMC, Samsung, and Intel. Commerce Secretary Howard Lutnick proposed taking a 10% stake in Intel. This could alter the terms of $33+ billion in finalized awards and create uncertainty for ongoing projects.
