Key Risks

Investment Thesis

Wolfspeed emerged from Chapter 11 bankruptcy on September 29, 2025 after filing on June 30, 2025. The restructuring reduced total debt by 70% (from $6.5B to ~$2B) and lowered annual interest expense by 60%. The $750M CHIPS Act award announced October 2024 was never disbursed under either the Biden or Trump administrations. The company's $5B Chatham County materials plant remains in planning stages with no construction timeline. Wolfspeed now operates with a self-funded business plan focused on its 200mm silicon carbide manufacturing footprint. (Sources: Wolfspeed press release Sep 29, 2025; Manufacturing Dive Jul 2, 2025; Commerce.gov CHIPS data)
