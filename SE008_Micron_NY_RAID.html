Investment Thesis

Micron's Clay, New York megafab has experienced significant 2-3 year schedule delays per the Final EIS accepted November 12, 2025. The project at White Pine Commerce Park (1,377 acres) will be the largest domestic producer of DRAM chips when complete. Construction start pushed from late 2025 to Q2 2026. Fab 1 now targets Q3 2030 (was 2028). Fab 2 targets Q4 2033 (was 2030). Full buildout of all 4 fabs extends to 2041 (was 2040). NYSDEC issued 9 environmental permits December 12, 2025 enabling construction. Approximately $1.2B in CHIPS funding reallocated to accelerate Micron's ID2 fab in Boise, Idaho. County Executive McMahon called this the "worst-case-scenario timeline" reflecting industry-wide challenges with greenfield fabs. Community concerns over 49-year property tax agreement. Despite delays, Micron remains committed to largest private investment in NY history. CHIPS award of $6.165B finalized Dec 2024. NY providing $5.5B Green CHIPS incentives. Will create 9,000 direct jobs, 40,000+ construction and supply chain jobs. (Sources: NYSDEC Dec 2025, Final EIS Nov 2025, Tom's Hardware Nov 2025, Construction Dive Nov 2025, ENR Nov 2025)
